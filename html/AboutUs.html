About Us

Mobile world is a licensed importer and distributor of smartphones, telecom gadgets, and accessories in Sri Lanka. We offer the broadest range of telecom products from the leading manufacturers from across the world. Mobile world is a TRC (Telecommunications Regulatory Commission) Sri Lanka approved company and we comply with all the laws of the land to ensure complete transparency in our services. With a brilliant record of serving the business clients, Mobile world has nurtured a healthy clients’ matrix and we continue to offer satisfactory services.Mobile world was established in the year 2015 and has accumulated worthy experience at its credit towards ensuring quality and timeliness for the clients. Mobile world deals in the niches of mobile phones, smart home automation products, and personal use gadgets. With cleaner protocols of sourcing directly from the OEMs, our consignments never suffer through delays. Our ‘in house’ quality team does an inspection of every batch imported to eliminate the quality concerns for the sellers and users. With such dedication in service, Mobile world has become the preferred distributor of mobile phones and personal use electronic gadgets in Sri Lanka.
